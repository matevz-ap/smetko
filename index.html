How it works

Smetko aims to help those visually impaired recycle waste with the help of Google Vision API. The user interface is simple, with a big button for taking photos, and a center layout with camera preview.

After taking an image, the picture is sent to Google Vision API. Depending on the returned data, the display shows a colored screen corresponding to the color of the trash bin. Besides that, the app also gives an audio cue for those who are completely blind.

Why Smetko?

There are currently 285 million visually impaired people on this planet. 49 million of them are completely blind. A revolutionary android application for waste recycling helps users recycle waste expediently on a daily basis. The app is also suitable for visually impaired people - those, who struggle with recycling the most.

Whats next?

We think that the app could also be used by kids to learn about recycling. Taking a valid picture would grant the user some sort of coins or XP, and he/she could level up by recycling more. Children could also track their past progress, add friends and compete in recycling with them by building a good habit from a young age.
